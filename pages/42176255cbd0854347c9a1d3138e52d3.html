Lines and Patterns
\n

Part of our brand style includes the use of lines and patterns. Patterns add texture and depth, while lines can guide a reader\'s eye to content.

Photography
\n

The photos you use can have a significant impact on the effectiveness of your marketing piece. We offer professional photos on Flickr free of charge, or you can make a photo request with our staff photographer.

Fonts
\n

Our fonts are Orgon Slab, Tungsten and TSTAR Mono Round. Orgon Slab is installed on all registered campus computers. Tungsten and TSTAR Mono Round are available by special request.
