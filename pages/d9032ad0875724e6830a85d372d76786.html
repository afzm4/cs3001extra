Buehler Hall

\n\t\t\t\n\t\t
\n\t
\n
\n\n\n\n
\n\t
\n\t\t
\n\n\t\t\t
\n
Buehler Hall is an apartment style complex made up of 6 to 12 person units. Each unit contains a common living area, dining room and full size kitchen for you to use.
\n
\n\n\t\t
\n\t
\n
\n\n\n\n
\n\t
\n\t\t
\n\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Room Features

\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t
    \n
  • One extra long twin bed with matress pad per student
    • \n
  • One dresser, desk and chair per student
    • \n
  • Cable and internet access (See Cable section below for
    more details)
    • \n
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Suite Features

\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t
    \n
  • Kitchen complete with full-sized fridge, oven, dishwasher, microwave, table and chairs
    • \n
  • Two to four bathrooms per suite
    • \n
  • Living room with a couch, chairs and entertainment/TV stand
    • \n
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t
\n\t\t
\n\t
\n
\n\n\n\n
\n\n\t
\n\t\t
\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

You will have cable TV in their common area, and can request cable in your rooms upon move-in.
