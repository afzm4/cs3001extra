Our Career Fair is twice a year

\n

On average, 300 companies attend the event to network with 2,500 students and alumni seeking full-time employment, co-ops and internships. Career fairs are held during the fall and spring semesters at Missouri S&T, but employers visit the campus year-round for interviews.

Making connections

\n

The Mentor a Miner program is a networking tool designed to connect students or alumni with professors, other alumni or professionals who have industry knowledge and connections relevant to a field of study. The program gives our students an extra advantage, whether it be through educational assistance, industry insight or career advising.

A Helping Hand

\n

Approximately 500 students utilize the suit closet each year at Missouri S&T.  By supplying students with professional attire, we are giving them a better chance of obtaining a full-time job, co-op or internship.

Event Calendar

\n\n
\n
\n
\n\n\n\n
\n\n\t
\n\t\t
\n\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

The Etiquette Dinner is open to all students (freshman through graduate). It includes a 5 course meal where all of the fundamentals of proper dining etiquette will be explained. This is excellent training to have before dining with a potential employer. Reservations are limited based on space available. Transportation is not provided by the university or COER. There is a $15 reservation fee that is applied to your student account to cover partial cost of the meal. (COER covers 50% of the meal costs to subsidize the training.) Reservations cancelled after the RSVP date and no-shows will be subject to an additional $15 fee to cover the full cost of the meal.

\n

Wednesday, October 24th at 5:00pm to 7:00pm
Havener Center, St. Pat\'s Ballroom
1346 N. Bishop Ave., Rolla, MO 65401

\n

Registration opens September 1st, 2018

\n

You will need to log into HireMiners to sign up. 

\n

Please RSVP by October 15th, 2018
