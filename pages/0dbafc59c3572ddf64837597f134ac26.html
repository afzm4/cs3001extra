Enable JavaScript to see Google Maps

To use Google Maps, you need to turn on JavaScript in your browser, like Chrome or Firefox. If you don\xe2\x80\x99t have JavaScript turned on, you\xe2\x80\x99ll see an empty page when you go to Google Maps.

\n\n

To enable JavaScript from your computer:

\n\n

Google Chrome

\n\n
    \n
  1. Open a Chrome browser window, then click More .
    2. \n
  2. Select Settings.
    3. \n
  3. At the bottom, click Advanced.
    4. \n
  4. Under "Privacy and security", click Content settings.
    5. \n
  5. Click JavaScript.
    6. \n
  6. Turn on Allowed.
    7. \n
